The sum of the elements of the triple $(a,b,c)$ of non-zero real numbers that satisfies the system of the equations
    \begin{align*}
        \begin{cases}
            (b+c)^{2024} = 4a\\
            (a+c)^{2024} = 4b\\
            (a+b)^{2024} = 4c
        \end{cases}
    \end{align*}
    can be written as $\frac{x}{y^{\frac{2022}{2023}}}$, where $x$, $y$ are coprime. Find $x+y$.
