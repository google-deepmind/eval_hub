By the AM-GM we have:
\begin{eqnarray*}
1=4 \cdot \left(4^{x^2-2x-y-z+2}+4^{y^2-2y-z-t+2}+4^{z^2-2z-t-x+2}+4^{t^2-2t-x-y+2}\right) \\ \geq 4 \cdot 4 \sqrt[4]{4^{x^2-2x-y-z+2+y^2-2y-z-t+2+z^2-2z-t-x+2+t^2-2t-x-y+2}}\\=
\sqrt[4]{4^{x^2-2x-y-z+2+y^2-2y-z-t+2+z^2-2z-t-x+2+t^2-2t-x-y+2+8}} \\
\sqrt[4]{4^{(x-2)^2+(y-2)^2+(z-2)^2+(t-2)^2}} \geq \sqrt[4]{4^{0}}=1
\end{eqnarray*}
\noindent so we obtain $(x-2)^2+(y-2)^2+(z-2)^2+(t-2)^2=0$ and the only possible solution is $(x,y,z,t)=(2,2,2,2)$.
