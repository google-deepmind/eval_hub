10 people took part in a test consisting of 5 questions, Bernadette was one of the participants. The method of grading this test was as follows. If $k$ people answered correctly to a certain question, then every one of them was given $12-k$ points, and other participants were given 0 points. It turned out that the sum of all scores from this test was 116. The score of Bernadette was 32. What is the biggest possible number of questions that she might have answered correctly?
