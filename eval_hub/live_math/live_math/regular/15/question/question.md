The number of participants in the party is a 4-digit number starting with the digit $5$. Each participant is of exactly one of the following five nationalities: Azerbaijani, Bolivian, Canadian, Danish or Eritrean. It turned out that each participant of the party knows exactly a hundred Azerbaijani, a hundred and two Bolivians, a hundred and seventeen Canadians, a hundred and thirty-four Danes, and two hundred Eritreans among the other participants. Find the number of participants of the party.
