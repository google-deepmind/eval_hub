By Pythagorean theorem we have 
 $$KL^2+LM^2+MN^2+NK^2 = AK^2 + AN^2 + BK^2 + BL^2 + CL^2 + CM^2 + DM^2 + DN^2.$$
 Now 
 $$\frac{AK^2 + BK^2}{2} \ge \left(\frac{AK+BK}{2}\right)^2 = 12^2 = 144.$$
 Hence $AK^2 + BK^2 \ge 288$, and analogously $BL^2+LC^2 \ge 288$, $CM^2+MD^2\ge 288$, $DN^2+NA^2\ge 288$. Summing it all up together, we get 
 $$KL^2+LM^2+MN^2+NK^2 \ge 1152.$$
 
We get the equality when all $K$, $L$, $M$, $N$ are in the middles of the sides.
