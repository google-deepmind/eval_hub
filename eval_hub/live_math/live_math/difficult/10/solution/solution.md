Notice that:
$$1+3+\cdots+(n-1)=\frac{n^2}{4}$$
\noindent and:
$$2+4+\cdots +n=\frac{n(n+2)}{4}$$
\noindent so the expression is equivalent to:
$$\left(5^{\frac{n^2}{4}} \cdot 2^{\frac{n^2+2n}{4}} \right)^{\frac{1}{2024}} \cdot \left( \frac{1}{2}\right)^{\frac{n}{4048}}=10^{\frac{n^2}{4 \cdot 2024}}$$
\noindent so the expression is greater than $10$ if and only if $n^2 > 4 \cdot 2024$ if and only if $n \geq 90$. Hence the least $n$ is $90$.
